Services

My job as a personal trainer is to help my clients reach their physical goals. These different services allow my customers to choose from many different options to help them achieve those goals and reach their highest potential.

Strengh Training

If your goal is to gain muscle mass and bump up your max numbers, this option is for you! This option gives clients access to workout plans from many different professional powerlifters' routines that have been proven to increase strenth dramatically.

Endurance Training

If your goal is more repetitions and looking lean, this program is for you! This program focuses more on building individual muscles instead of focusing on how they work together when you are strength training. This program takes routines from professional body builders to help you achieve your goals.

Longevity Training

This program is a lot simpler and a lot more beneficial in the long run. This program will focus on the muscles working as a group but instead of having the muscles work together to move the heaviest weight possible as they do during strength training, this program will focus more on how they work together to create a body that looks good and feels good for a long time.

Muscle Education

This option is for clients who want to take the next step in their fitness journey and learn more about how the body works. With the help of profesional kinesiologists, clients will be able to take a course to learn the basics of how the body functions.

Diet/Meal Planning

The biggest part of creating a strong, functional body is diet. This option will allow the client to work with dietitions to create a custom diet that can be passed along to our professional chefs that will create pre made meals to make your diet goals that much easier.
